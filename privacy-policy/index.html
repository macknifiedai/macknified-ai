Legal Basis for Processing Under GDPR

Where GDPR applies, we process personal data based on one or more of the following legal grounds:

Data Retention

We retain personal data only for as long as reasonably necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

Our general retention practices may include:

  • Account Data: Retained while your account is active and for up to 30 days after a verified deletion request, subject to legal, billing, security, and backup requirements.
  • Chat and Conversation Data: Retained for up to two years or until you request deletion, unless a different retention period is disclosed for a particular feature.
  • Payment Records: Retained for up to seven years or as required for tax, accounting, fraud prevention, and legal-compliance purposes.
  • Analytics Data: Aggregated, anonymized, or deleted when no longer reasonably necessary for analysis and service improvement.
  • Marketing Consent Records: Retained as necessary to document consent, honor opt-out requests, and comply with applicable laws.

We may retain de-identified, aggregated, or anonymized information for lawful business purposes where it cannot reasonably identify you.

Google API Services — Limited Use

Macknified AI's use and transfer of information received from Google APIs complies with the Google API Services User Data Policy, including applicable Limited Use requirements.

Information obtained from Google APIs, including Google Calendar, Sheets, Docs, Drive, and Gmail, is used solely to provide or improve the user-facing feature you have enabled. We do not sell Google user data, use it for advertising purposes, or use it to train generalized artificial intelligence or machine-learning models.
